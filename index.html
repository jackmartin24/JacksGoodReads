Welcome

Hello, welcome to my site. My name is Jack and I like to read books so I decided to create my site based off of some books that I enjoyed to read. I mainly read sci-fi and fantasy books so the reviews will all be about those genres and if I can remember how the book made me feel. I will also be giving a summary which I get from this site. If the book I am reviewing is part of a series the summary will only be of the first book but I will give my review to the series as a whole.
